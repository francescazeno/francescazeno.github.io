Timeline

      1838 - Stereopsis
      1935 - Pygmalion's Spectables
      1956 - Sensorama
      1960 - Telesphere Mask
      1961 - Headsight
      1965 - Ultimate Display
      1966 - Flight Simulator
      1968 - The Sword of Damocles
      1969 - Myron Krueger
      1972 - GE Flight Simulator w/ 180
      1975 - VIDEOPLACE
      1977 - Aspen Movie Map
      1979 - VITAL helmet
      1980 - Stereo Vision Glasses
      1982 - Sayre Gloves
      1985 - VPL Research, Inc. Founded
      1986 - The Super Cockpit Flight Simulator
      1987 - The Virtul Cockpit FLight Simulator
      1989 - Crystal River Engineering - VIEW
      1990 - Virtuality VR Arcade Machine
      1991 - Computer Simulated Teleoperation - NASA
      1994 - SEGA VR-1 & VictorMaxx CyberMaxx
      1995 - Nintendo Virtual Boy & Virtual IO I-Glasses & VFX1 Headgear Forte
      1997 - Virtual Vietnam
      2001 - SAS Cube which led to Virtools VR PAck
      2007 - Google Street View - Immersive Media captured that footage
      2010 - Google stereoscopic 3D Street View & Palmer Luckey, 18 yr old, created first prototype for Oculus Rift
      2012 - Oculus Kickstarter raised $2.4 million
      2014 - Facebook bought Oculus for $2 Billion & Sony announced Project Morpheus & Google - Cardboard & Samsung - Samsung Gear VR
      2015 - Wall Street Journal launched VR roller coaster & BBC 360 video migrant camp & Washington Post VR expereince of Oval Office & RYOT vr film & Gloveone had successful kickstarter campaign
      2016 - Most headsets had dynamic binaural audio & Haptic interfaces underdeveloped & HTC released HTC VIVE Steam VR headset w/ first commercial release of headset w/ sensor-based tracking
      2017 - Several companies developing VR including HTC, Google, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, Sony, Samsung, etc
      2018 - Facebook demonstrated new prototype, the Half Dome.
      2019 - Oculus Rift S, Oculus Quest no cables, Quest can connect to pc, vive cosmos, pro eye, focus plus, Valve Index
